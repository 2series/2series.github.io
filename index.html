Biography

As a data scientist, Rihad plans and leads the development of new data analytic techniques, methodologies and analytical solutions from design through to implementation, prototyping and testing. Rihad identifies and develops core data science components for the delivery of projects and computing environments. Rihad explores and visualises complex data set to provide incremental business value. Rihad builds data manipulation and data processing tools, creates models and algorithms, proposes and adopts new technologies. He provides technical input to client proposals and commercial activities.

Rihad has the ability to adopt a broader perspective and display analytical thinking to identify connections between concepts. He is able to influence key stakeholders and spearhead a data driven approach to resolve business issues. He builds a strategy roadmap to ensure analytics products and development activities are synergistic and leads to a longer term sustainable outcome.
